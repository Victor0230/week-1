About Us

Welcome to Claybrook Zoo, a place where nature and wildlife come together to create unforgettable experiences! Located in the heart of the city, our zoo is home to over 1,000 animals from around the world, including lions, elephants, giraffes, and many more.

Come and join us for a day of adventure, discovery, and fun. We can't wait to see you at Claybrook Zoo!

Our zoo has the largest lion enclosure in the country!
